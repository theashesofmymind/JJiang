Crabird

2024
14 * 16 * 14 inches

king crabs, duck feathers, bells, lights, isolation coating, spray paint, wire, cotton


Crabird envisions an adapted species born in a post-human-centric era of evolution.
Drawing from materials often relegated to human consumption and comfort—king crabs served at tables, duck feathers tucked into pillows, the work reimagines the roles of organic and artificial components in a world where interspecies relationships evolve beyond human control.
